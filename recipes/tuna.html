Tuna Tataki キハダ鮪のたたき

Description

Tuna Tataki is yellowfin/ahi tuna lightly seared and served rare with a citrus-based soy sauce. It’s very easy and quick to prepare, and it is a light yet flavorful appetizer to start a meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb sashimi-grade yellowfin/ahi tuna
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Sauce

  • 1 green chopped onion
  • 1 knob grate ginger
  • 3 tbsp ponzu
  • 2 tsp roasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp toasted white sesame seeds

Garnish

  • 1/2 lemon, sliced

Steps

  1. Grate ginger and slice green onion thinly.
  2. Combine the tataki sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  3. Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan. When the oil is hot, sear the tuna 30 seconds on each side.
  4. When all sides are seared, remove from the heat and let it cool. Slice the tuna into ¼ inch (6 mm) pieces. Pour the sauce and serve.
