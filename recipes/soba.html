Zaru Soba ざるそば

Description

Chilled Soba Noodles, or Zaru Soba, is a classic summertime meal in Japan accompanied by a savory dipping sauce called tsuyu, and a plethora of condiments called yakumi.

Ingredients

Soba

  • 7 oz soba noodles
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • shredded nori
  • green onion
  • wasabi

Mentsuyu Dipping Sauce

  • 1 cup dashi
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Add dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar into a saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring to ensure the sauce is well combined. Set aside to cool, or place in the freezer to speed up the process.
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse the noodles in ice-cold water tossing them to cool evenly, and drain them of excess water. Garnish as desired.
  3. Pour the dipping sauce into a small bowl and dress to your liking. Dip your noodles in the sauce and enjoy!
